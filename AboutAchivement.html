About Achievement Awards

10th Annual 2025 Globee® Awards for Achievement

We hear you—you want to participate, and we’re making that possible.

Formerly known as the Globee® Awards for American Business, the program is evolving into the Globee® Awards for Achievement, and starting in 2026, individuals, teams, and organizations from all over the world will be welcome to participate.

The Globee® Awards for Achievement has expanded its scope to recognize an even wider array of achievements. This unique awards program remains unparalleled in its dedication to celebrating outstanding accomplishments across various categories within American business. There is no other program like this, making it a distinctive platform for acknowledging business excellence and innovation throughout the United States.

A prestigious global awards program recognizing individuals, teams, and organizations worldwide for their outstanding achievements in business and the workplace.

About Globee® Awards for Achievement

All entry submissions must be made exclusively through our online platform. We strongly recommend including supporting materials such as news articles, press releases, social media links, website URLs (e.g., references, testimonials), and attachments (e.g., documents, presentation slides, videos) to substantiate your submission. Only publicly available and published materials should be submitted. Do not submit any private, confidential, or NDA-protected content. Furthermore, any supporting materials submitted must not be password protected or require a login to view by the judges. All supporting materials can be uploaded digitally as website links or attachments through the platform. Physical materials are not accepted; we only accept digital submissions.

Please register using your work/business email address only.

Nominations are evaluated using a structured, data-driven scoring system to ensure fairness and consistency. Judges score each entry across four predefined areas:

  1. Achievement – the nominee’s most significant accomplishment;
  2. Main Content – the clarity, depth, and relevance of the essay or explanation;
  3. Summary – the conciseness and strength of the key points explaining why the nomination deserves recognition; and
  4. Supporting Materials – the credibility, accessibility, and relevance of the publicly available supporting materials submitted.

This comprehensive evaluation process ensures that every nomination is reviewed thoroughly and objectively based on its merit and impact.

Providing publicly viewable supporting materials is a key requirement for your nomination to be considered.

To get started, please create your account using your work/business email address. Please do not use personal or generic domain emails for applying. Also please note that using multiple email addresses will result in delays in your acceptance process.
